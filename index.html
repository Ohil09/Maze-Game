TWISTED TRAILS

Description

Navigate the twists. Outsmart the traps. Guide your character through mind-bending mazes using simple command moves. Every level is a puzzle—plan smart, move sharp, and reach the goal with precision!

Rules

  • Drag commands into the Solution box.
  • Use Move Forward, Turn Left, and Turn Right.
  • The sequence of the command cards should make the character reach the end of the puzzle
  • You have limited time in each level.
  • Hitting a wall reduces a chance. Lose all 3 → fail.
  • Reaching the goal completes the level and gives score.
  • Click Preview to see the game layout.
