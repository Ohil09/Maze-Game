Twisted Trails

Navigate the twists. Outsmart the traps.

Guide your character through mind-bending mazes using command cards. Plan smart, move sharp, and reach the goal with precision!

How to Play

⟶
Drag or click command cards into the Solution box
▶
Use Move, Turn Left, and Turn Right
⏱
Beat the clock — each level has a time limit
✕
Hitting a wall costs a chance. Lose all 3 and it's over
★
Reach the trophy to complete the level and earn points
